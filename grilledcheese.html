Grilled Cheese

Comin at y'all with another easy af recipe

Best enjoyed on a cold night after partying. Enjoy!

Ingredients

    1 tbsp mayonnaise
    2 slices bread
    2 slices of white cheddar Cheese

Directions

    Step 1 - Spread mayo on bread and place it mayo side down into hot skillet. Add cheese
    Step 2 - Spread other slice of bread with mayo and place mayo side up on top of cheese
    Step 3 - Cook until lightly browned on one side. FLip over and cook until cheese is melted
